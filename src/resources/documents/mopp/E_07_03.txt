7.3.1 Financial assistance to students 
Counselling Services (Student Support Services Department) provides student counselling on issues relating to financial well-being and advice on the various types of financial assistance available to students.
Details of Australian Government assistance for students (ie Youth Allowance, Austudy and Abstudy) can be found on the 
Centrelink website
.  Details of the Government's Commonwealth Learning Scholarship program, and scholarships and assistance provided by QUT, can be found on the QUT Scholarships website.
Top 
7.3.2 Student Financial Assistance Scheme 
QUT's Student Financial Assistance Scheme (SFAS) is a small loans scheme which 
assists students who are in short term financial hardship. It is administered 
by the Student Financial Assistance Scheme Committee (see  
E/7.4
Further details on eligibility and application procedures for 
SFAS 
  loans
can be found on the Counselling Services website. Procedures relating 
  to the administration of SFAS loans are set in the 
Financial 
  Procedures Manual, Chapter 2, Section I
