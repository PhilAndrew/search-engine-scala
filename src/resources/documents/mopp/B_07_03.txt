7.3.1 Salary levels 
Salary levels are contained in the relevant Enterprise Agreements as follows
QUT Enterprise Agreement (Professional Staff)
QUT Enterprise Agreement (Academic Staff)
QUT  Employee Collective Agreement (Senior Staff) 2009 - 2012.
Top 
7.3.2 Payment of salaries 
Salaries are paid into financial institution accounts on a fortnightly basis. Relevant forms and further
  information are available from the 
Finance and Resource Planning
website.
Top 
7.3.3 Allowances 
Provisions relating to the following allowances for professional staff are contained in the QUT 
Enterprise
      Agreement (Professional Staff)
higher duties allowance
meal allowances during overtime
first aid allowance
sanitary allowance
tool allowance
field trip allowance
mileage for use of privately owned vehicles.
Provisions relating to the payment of higher duties
  allowance for academic staff are contained in the 
QUT
Enterprise Agreement (Academic Staff).
The following allowances are available to staff
  in appropriate circumstances, and provisions for payment of the allowances, including amounts payable,
  are available from the 
Human Resources
  Department
website
Travelling - Australia and international - all staff
Mileage for use of privately owned vehicles – all staff
Travel away from home allowance(PDL) and limits on private income - academic staff only
Field trip allowance for academic staff (for field trip allowance for professional staff, see above)
Settling-in allowance – all staff.
Travel and mileage allowances are in accordance with the rates  established by the Australian Taxation Office. Other allowances are varied from  time to time by the Vice-Chancellor. 
Top 
7.3.4 Flexible Remuneration Scheme 
The parameters of the flexible  remuneration scheme are contained in the Enterprise Agreements as  follows
for academic staff, the 
QUT Enterprise Agreement (Academic Staff)
for professional staff, the 
QUT Enterprise Agreement (Professional Staff)
for senior staff, the 
QUT Employee Collective Agreement (Senior Staff) 2009 - 2012.
Information on operational  aspects of the scheme is available from the 
Corporate Finance
website.
For academic and professional staff, there is a  50% maximum of  total employment cost or total remuneration for salary sacrificing all  benefits, except for superannuation where there is no maximum. Information on  salary sacrificing of superannuation is available from the 
Human Resources website.
