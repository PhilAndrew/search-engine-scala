8.2.1 Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Committee 
Aboriginal and Torres  Strait Islander Committee is a Vice-Chancellor's committee established in  accordance with Council Procedure 1 - Committees .  Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Committee is accountable to the Deputy  Vice-Chancellor (Academic) for fulfilling the following terms of reference.
Terms of reference
Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Committee:
  facilitates development of strategic relationships with  Indigenous communities and local groups; 
  advises management, through Chancellery, Oodgeroo Unit,  Equity Services, or through committees such as University Teaching and Learning  Committee and University Research and Innovation Committee on: 
    implementation of the QUT Reconciliation Statement; 
    inclusion of Indigenous perspectives in curriculum; 
    recruitment and support for Indigenous students; 
    appropriate pursuit of research involving and   impacting on Indigenous people; 
    any other matter affecting Indigenous people at QUT. 
Membership
  One distinguished Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander  person as Chair, nominated by the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Academic) on  advice from Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Committee 
  Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Academic) ex officio 
  Manager, Oodgeroo Unit ex officio 
  One student nominated by the Vice-Chancellor 
  Two members elected by and from the academic staff of  the Oodgeroo Unit 
  Two Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander staff of the  University, other than from the Oodgeroo Unit, nominated by Aboriginal and  Torres Strait Islander Committee 
  One nominee of University Academic Board who is a  member of Council 
  Equity Director or nominee 
  One nominee of DEEWR (Department of Education, Employment and Workplace Relations) 
  One nominee of the Aboriginal and Torres Strait  Islander Commission (South-East Queensland Indigenous Regional Council) 
  Two representatives from Aboriginal and/or Torres  Strait Islander organisations nominated by Aboriginal and Torres Strait  Islander Committee 
  One nominee of the Queensland Department of Education 
A nominee of the Registrar is secretary.
The Manager, Oodgeroo  Unit acts as executive officer of the committee.
Tenure and frequency of meeting
Terms of office are as detailed in section 6 of 
Council Procedure 1 - Committees
The Aboriginal and Torres  Strait Islander Committee meets at least four times a year.
Reporting
Aboriginal and Torres  Strait Islander Committee reports to the Vice-Chancellor at least annually.
