Student Financial Assistance Scheme Committee is a Vice-Chancellor's committee established in accordance with 
  Council Procedure 1 - Committees
  . Student Financial Assistance Scheme Committee is accountable
  to the Registrar for fulfilling the following terms of reference.
Terms of reference
Student Financial  Assistance Scheme Committee:
  examines, organises and offers comment as appropriate in respect of any facet of the day to day operation of the Student Financial Assistance Scheme (see E/7.3.2 );
  on delegated authority of the Vice-Chancellor and subject to fund availability, makes loans to students experiencing financial  hardship, ratifies Counsellor loans, approves extensions to loans, determines  interest rates and imposes penalties;
  makes recommendations to the Registrar on all matters of policy relating to the scheme;
  makes recommendation to the Director, Corporate Finance, that loans identified as non-recoverable be written-off;
  considers  loan applications that fall outside the SFAS Loan categories and criteria in exceptional circumstances.
Membership
  Director, Student Support Services or nominee as Chair
  Head, Counselling Services or nominee 
  Welfare Officer, Counselling Services
  Director, Corporate Finance or nominee
  Head, International Student Services or nominee
  Welfare Officer, International Student Services
  One counsellor / loan advisor from Counselling  Services
A nominee of the Registrar  is secretary, usually from the Corporate Finance Department.
Tenure and  frequency of meeting
Terms of office are as  detailed in section 6 of Council Procedure 1 -  Committees.
Student Financial  Assistance Scheme Committee meets fortnightly, or as required.
Reporting
Student Financial  Assistance Scheme Committee reports to the Registrar, at least annually.
