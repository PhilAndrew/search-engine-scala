International Leaders Group is a  Vice-Chancellor's committee established in accordance with Council Procedure 1 - Committees and is  accountable to the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (International and Development) for  fulfilling the following terms of reference. 
Terms of reference
International Leaders Group brings  together academic international leaders with their divisional counterparts for  the purpose of sharing information about international initiatives, providing  advice on international strategic and policy matters and implementation of the  annual International Engagement and Recruitment Plan. 
International Leaders Group
  provides a forum for information sharing on international  initiatives and activities
  advises on policy, planning and strategic matters and makes  recommendations to International Steering Committee (I/2.1) 
  provides a forum for coordination of international activities across the  University 
  assists in the development and implementation of policies,  programs and projects and makes recommendations to International Steering  Committee as required 
  discusses and provides advice on emerging issues or innovations,  and
  discusses and provides advice on policy initiatives taken by the  University, government and other external agencies relating to international  students.
Membership
  Deputy Vice-Chancellor (International and Development) ex  officio as Chair 
  Assistant deans or directors of international from each faculty or  equivalent as nominated by the executive dean of each faculty
  Dean of Research and Research Training ex  officio
  Director, Office of Commercial Services ex officio
  Manager, International Student Services ex officio 
  Manager, Admissions, Student Business Services ex  officio 
  International Careers Counsellor ex officio
  Executive Director, QUT International ex officio
  Director, QUT   International College ex officio 
  Director,  International Marketing and Recruitment ex officio
  Director, International Business Development, ex officio
A nominee of the Deputy  Vice-Chancellor (International and Development) is Secretary. 
Tenure and frequency of meeting
Terms of office are as detailed in section 6 of Council Procedure 1 - Committees.
International Leaders Group meets six times per  year. 
Reporting
International Leaders Group reports to  International Steering Committee via the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (International  and Development).
