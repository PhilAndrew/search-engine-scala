4.11.1 New Professors Scheme grant 
As an  initiative of the Vice-Chancellor, new professors in the University are  eligible for a one-off grant of $25 000 which may be used for a specific  project or projects within the University. Amounts in excess of this may be  authorised under exceptional circumstances at the discretion of the  Vice-Chancellor.
Top
4.11.2 Eligibility 
Staff appointed to the level  of professor in the University, and who have not previously held this position  at the University, are eligible to receive a New Professors Scheme grant.  Appointment may be as a result of a recruitment process, a promotion process or  an appointment by invitation.
Top
4.11.3 Grant expenditure 
Expenditure  of the grant must be seen to benefit the grant recipient's professional  development and the faculty/ institute to which the recipient belongs. A brief  project proposal submitted through the relevant executive dean/ executive  director must be approved by the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Academic) before grant  funds can be expended. Examples for which the grant may be used include purchase  of equipment, or hiring of staff to facilitate the project.
