Modification History
Student  Misconduct Committee is a Vice-Chancellor's committee established in accordance  with Council Procedure 1 -  Committees and E/8.1 Management of student discipline.  Student Misconduct Committee is accountable to the Vice-Chancellor for  fulfilling the following terms of reference.
Terms of  reference 
  Student  Misconduct Committee 
  investigates  and hears cases of student misconduct referred to it by the Deputy  Vice-Chancellor (Technology, Information and Learning Support), an executive  dean or the Security Manager
  recommends  to the Registrar on its findings of whether or not a student should be found  guilty of misconduct and any penalties to be applied.
Membership
  Two senior academic staff members nominated by the  Vice-Chancellor
  One professional staff member with experience in  student administration nominated by the Vice-Chancellor
  Two students nominated by the Student Guild on each  occasion on which Student Misconduct Committee is convened to deal with a  matter. 
The Chair shall  be nominated by the Vice-Chancellor.
  A nominee  of the Registrar is secretary.
Quorum
  The quorum  for Student Misconduct Committee is three members.
Procedures for conduct of Student Misconduct  Committee business
  Hearings of  cases of student misconduct by Student Misconduct Committee will be conducted  in accordance with E/8.1 Management of student discipline. 
Tenure and  frequency of meeting 
  Terms of  office are detailed in section 6 of Council Procedure 1 -  Committees .
  Student  Misconduct Committee meets as required.
Reporting 
  After each  meeting, Student Misconduct Committee reports to the Registrar on its findings.  Student Misconduct Committee reports on an annual basis to the Vice-Chancellor.
