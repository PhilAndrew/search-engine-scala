Modification History 
Engagement Committee is a Vice-Chancellor's  committee established in accordance with Council Procedure 1 &ndash; Committees.  Engagement Committee is accountable to the Vice-Chancellor for fulfilling the  following terms of reference.
Terms of reference
Engagement  Committee
  provides advice to the       Vice-Chancellor, through Vice-Chancellor&rsquo;s Advisory Committee, on 
       engagement        priorities / directions, including major partnerships and initiatives 
       engagement        components of University, faculty, institute and division plans
       implications        of trends and developments in engagement, nationally and internationally
       opportunities        for deepening and sharing engagement knowledge
       coordination        of cross-faculty / institute / division collaboration on engagement        initiatives&nbsp;
       progress        of faculties, institutes and divisions against the Engagement Strategy
       recommended        revisions to the Engagement Strategy in line with top-level plans and the        QUT Blueprint
     guides and monitors activities       of engagement project teams and/or associated working parties
     provides oversight and       annual review of the Engagement Incentive Fund Scheme
     considers other matters       relating to engagement as referred to it by the Vice-Chancellor or Deputy       Vice-Chancellor (International and Development).
Membership
  Deputy Vice-Chancellor (International and Development) ex officio as Chair
  Two Executive Deans nominated by Vice-Chancellor&rsquo;s  Advisory Committee
  One Institute Director nominated by the Deputy  Vice-Chancellor (Research and Commercialisation)
  One Assistant Dean (Research) nominated by the Deputy  Vice-Chancellor (Research and Commercialisation)
  One Assistant Dean (Teaching and Learning) nominated  by the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Learning and Teaching)
  One Assistant Dean, External Relations (or equivalent)  nominated by the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (International and Development)
  Two additional Assistant Deans nominated by the Deputy  Vice-Chancellor (International and Development) to ensure representation from  each faculty
  One senior staff member from the Division of Research  and Commercialisation nominated by the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Research and  Commercialisation)
  One senior staff member from the Learning and Teaching Unit nominated by the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Learning and Teaching)
  One external member with expertise or interest in  engagement, nominated by Deputy Vice-Chancellor (International and Development)
  Head of Caboolture Campus ex officio
  Director, Alumni and Development Office ex officio
A nominee of the Registrar is  secretary.
Tenure and frequency of meeting
Terms of office are as detailed in section 6 of 
Council Procedure
  1 - Committees
Engagement Committee normally meets four  times a year.
