4.1.1 Publication design, production and print buying 
The University, through Visual Communication Services produces high-quality publications in line with QUT's corporate identity and marketing  and communication strategies, including 
  major corporate       publications, eg QUT Annual Report 
   brochures, from single page flyers       to major promotional publications 
   newsletters and stationery 
   editing / proof-reading of       text or the hiring of part-time writers for specific tasks 
   small run digital printing       of promotional material.
Design, typesetting, production and print buying of all marketing and promotional material is carried
out only through the Publications Unit.
Small  run digital printing or photocopying of non-promotional material, where no  design or typesetting is required, is handled directly through 
  QUT
  Printing Services .
Educational print materials are produced through QUT Printing Services.

4.1.2 Editorial quality 
The public's image of QUT is strongly affected by the editorial quality of material disseminated in
  the name of the University and high editorial standards of readability, accuracy and consistency are
  essential to promote QUT as a quality institution.
All QUT promotional material or publications for sale outside the University should be edited and
  proofed by a person with relevant tertiary qualifications or equivalent experience, who is not the
  same person responsible for writing the publication.

4.1.3 Corporate publications 
QUT Handbook
The Student Business Services Department is responsible for the content of the QUT Handbook which
  is regarded as the University's official publication of its governing structure and authority. The
  QUT Handbook includes
QUT's history, vision and organisational structure
principal dates, policy, rules and regulations relating to student matters
Council and committee details
senior staff list
academic and student support services
fees and administration charges
course structures and requirements
unit synopses.
The QUT Handbook is available on the internet via the QUT home page (
http://www.handbook.qut.edu.au
QUT Annual Report
The Marketing and Communication Department produces the QUT Annual Report, which gives an overview
  of QUT and its operation for the calendar year. The Annual Report satisfies statutory requirements
  and provides details of the University's financial performance and audited accounts.
