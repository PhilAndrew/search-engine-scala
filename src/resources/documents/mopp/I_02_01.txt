International Steering  Committee is a Vice-Chancellor's committee established in accordance with Council Procedure 1 - Committees . International  Steering Committee is accountable to the Vice-Chancellor for fulfilling the  following terms of reference.
Terms of reference
International Steering  Committee brings together a small group of members, nominated by  Vice-Chancellor's Advisory Committee and key senior management, to consider  international policy and planning issues prior to consideration by Vice-Chancellor's  Advisory Committee. International business is brought to Vice-Chancellor's  Advisory Committee via the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (International and  Development) who chairs International Steering Committee. Key areas of concern  to International Steering Committee are the University's international  marketing plans, strategic partnerships, international institutional relations  and quality assurance mechanisms. 
International Steering Committee has one major sub-committee, International  Leaders Group, which  convenes on a regular basis. 
Membership
  Deputy Vice-Chancellor       (International and Development) ex       officio as Chair 
  Deputy Vice-Chancellor       (Academic) or nominee 
  Registrar or nominee 
  Three executive deans nominated       by Vice-Chancellor's Advisory Committee
  One Assistant Dean       (International) nominated by International Leaders Group 
  Executive Director, QUT       International ex officio.
A nominee of  the Registrar is secretary 
Tenure and frequency of meeting
Terms of office  are as detailed in section 6 of Council Procedure 1 - Committees .
International  Steering Committee meets three to four times per year as required. 
Reporting
International Steering Committee reports to  Vice-Chancellor's Advisory Committee through the Deputy Vice-Chancellor  (International and Development) and interconnects with the International  Leaders Group.
