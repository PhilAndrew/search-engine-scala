1.1.1 Employment categories 
There are three broad categories of QUT staff:
Academic staff, which includes research only academic staff;
Professional staff, which includes
Administrative and clerical staff
Professional and technical staff
Trades and support staff
Research only professional staff; and
Senior staff, comprising  all positions classified Senior Staff Group 1 to Senior Staff Group 7 (SSG1 to  SSG7). For the purposes of Council elections, heads of school, executive deans  of faculty, directors of institutes, Deputy  Vice-Chancellor (Research and Commercialisation), Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Learning and Teaching) and Deputy  Vice-Chancellor (Academic) are deemed to be members of the academic staff.
Top 
1.1.2 Enterprise Agreements 
QUT staff  will  be employed under one of three enterprise agreements:
QUT Enterprise
        Bargaining Agreement (Professional Staff)
QUT Enterprise
        Bargaining Agreement (Academic Staff)
QUT Employee Collective Agreement (Senior Staff) 2009-2012
Each enterprise agreement contains the current conditions of employment for those staff covered by
  the agreement. Persons appointed to honorary and visiting appointments are excluded from the provisions
  of enterprise agreements
Top 
1.1.3 Relevant legislation 
There are a number of State and Commonwealth Acts of Parliament which impact upon the employment of
staff at QUT. These include:
Anti-Discrimination Act 1991 (Qld)
Disability Discrimination Act 1992 (Cwth)
Fair Work Act 2009 (Cwth)
  Human Rights and Equal Opportunity Commission Act 1986 (Cwth)
Racial Discrimination Act 1975 (Cwth)
    Sex Discrimination Act 1984
  (Cwth)
Workers' Compensation and Rehabilitation Act 2003 (Qld)
Other relevant Acts are referenced throughout MOPP Chapter B as they pertain to each policy matter.
