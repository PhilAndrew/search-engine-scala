    THIS POLICY IS CURRENTLY UNDER REVIEW -
    CONTACT Senior HUMAN RESOURCES Officer  FOR FURTHER DETAILS.
When academic staff are invited to accept membership of Australian or international 
  bodies (other than professional bodies) which will require travel, leave or 
  other costs to QUT, prior approval should be sought from the Vice-Chancellor 
  through the relevant head of school and executive dean of faculty. When seeking approval, 
  staff should indicate total costs involved.
The Vice-Chancellor funds approved travel from University funds. The University 
  does not however reimburse staff members for membership fees relating to these 
  bodies (see section
