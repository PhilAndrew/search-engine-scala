Modification HistoryStudent  Research Misconduct Committee is a Vice-Chancellor's committee established in  accordance with Council Procedure 1 -  Committees, D/2.7 Procedures for dealing with allegations of research misconduct and E/8.1 Management of student discipline. Student  Research Misconduct Committee is accountable to the Vice-Chancellor for  fulfilling the following terms of reference.
  Terms of  reference 
Student  Research Misconduct Committee
  investigates  and hears cases of student research misconduct referred to it by the Deputy  Vice-Chancellor (Research and Commercialisation)
  recommends  to the Registrar on its findings of whether or not a student should be found  guilty of research misconduct and recommends on any penalties to be applied. 
Membership
  Dean of Research and Research Training ex officio, as Chair
  One academic or senior staff member nominated by  Research Degrees Committee
  One representative from the relevant faculty  nominated by Research Degrees Committee
  One research higher degree student nominated by QUT  Postgraduate Students Association.
A nominee  of the Registrar is secretary.
  Quorum
  The quorum  for Student Research Misconduct Committee is all four members.
  Procedures for conduct of Student Research  Misconduct Committee business
  Hearings of  cases of student research misconduct by Student Research Misconduct Committee  will be conducted in accordance with D/2.7 Procedures for dealing with allegations of research misconduct and E/8.1 Management of student discipline. 
  Tenure and  frequency of meeting
  Terms of  office are detailed in section 6 of Council Procedure 1 -  Committees.
  Student  Research Misconduct Committee meets as required.
Reporting
After each meeting, Student Research Misconduct  Committee reports to the Registrar on its findings. Student Research Misconduct  Committee reports on an annual basis to the Vice-Chancellor.
