6.1.1 Types of leave 
The following types of leave are available to QUT staff:
Recreation leave (see 
B/6.2
Sick leave (see 
B/6.3
Long service leave (see 
B/6.4
Leave without pay (see 
B/6.5
Parental leave (including maternity, partner and adoption leave) (see 
B/6.6
Personal leave (see 
B/ 6.7
Defence Reserve Forces leave, jury service leave, special leave for State
    Emergency Services call-out and workplace relations training leave (see 
B/6.8
Staff may not be absent from QUT during their working hours without approval
of the relevant authorising officer.

6.1.2 Application for leave 
For all types of leave (with the exception of recreation leave for academic
  and senior staff), staff must make application either in writing or on-line via 
QUT
Virtual
, subject to the provisions of the relevant leave policy.
