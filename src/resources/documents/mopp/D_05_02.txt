Research Degrees Committee is a Council committee established in accordance with 
Council
    Procedure 1 - Committees
. Research Degrees
  Committee is accountable to 
University Research and Innovation
Committee
for fulfilling the following terms of reference.
Terms of reference
Research Degrees Committee:
develops and recommends to University Research and Innovation Committee to recommend to University
    Academic Board policies and procedures relating to research higher degree supervision, training and
    research in the University;
advises University Research and Innovation Committee on the provision and delivery of resources
    and facilities for research degree training and research;
in conjunction with Learning and Teaching Unit, eLearning Services, the Library, the Office of Research
    and other support departments, organises and conducts training and information sessions for research
    higher degree students and supervisors;
considers for approval, in accordance with University policy (
D/5.3
    recommendations from faculty academic boards regarding the admission, supervision and examination
    of research higher degree students;
monitors the progress, supervision and examination of research higher degree students within the
    University and reports on these matters to the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Research and Commercialisation);
determines the final outcome of Second Stage grievance processes  relating to research higher degree students;
monitors  supervisor accreditation within the University relating to research higher  degree supervision;
  considers applications and determines recipients for domestic postgraduate research scholarships
  and grants-in-aid awards;
  recommends to University Academic Board (or delegate) the award of research degrees to candidates,
  through Student Business Services;
considers nominations for QUT Outstanding Doctoral Thesis Awards and  recommends recipients to the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Research and  Commercialisation) for approval.
Membership
Dean of Research and Research Training 
ex officio
as Chair
Manager, Research Students Centre  
ex officio
Director, International Graduate Research ex officio
Four associate professors / professors nominated by the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Research and Commercialisation),
    where membership is constituted with due regard for breadth of disciplinary representation
Five members with experience as supervisors of research higher degree students nominated by University
    Research and Innovation Committee, where membership is constituted with due regard for breadth of
    disciplinary representation
One postgraduate student nominated by QUT Postgraduate Students Association
A nominee of the Registrar is secretary, usually a nominee of the Dean of Research and Research Training.
Tenure and frequency of meeting
Terms of office are as detailed in section 6 of 
Council Procedure
  1 - Committees
Research Degrees Committee meets on at least a monthly basis or as required throughout the year.
Reporting
Following each meeting, Research Degrees Committee reports to the next meeting of University Research
  and Innovation Committee.
