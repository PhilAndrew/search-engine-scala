2.1.1 Appointment as Professor Emeritus 
Council may by resolution, and in recognition of distinguished academic service to QUT, confer the
  title of Professor Emeritus. Distinguished academic service in this context includes a professor awarded
  the title of Distinguished Professor (see policy 
B/2.2
) and normally those with 10 years or more service
as a professor or equivalent.
A Professor about to retire or resign, or a former Professor of QUT, will be given the title of Professor
  Emeritus. A Distinguished Professor about to retire or resign, or a former Distinguished Professor
  of QUT, will be given the title of Distinguished Professor Emeritus.
In cases where the title is conferred on a staff member about to retire or resign, the title will
  apply from the date of retirement or resignation.
A Distinguished Professor Emeritus or Professor Emeritus is regarded as a Professor of QUT for all
  ceremonial purposes.
