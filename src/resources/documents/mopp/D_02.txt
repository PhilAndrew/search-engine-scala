QUT's research goal is "to advance and apply knowledge germane
to the professions and to the communities with which QUT
interacts and relevant to the enhancement of economic, cultural
and social conditions" (see section 
A/2
In the context of its vision and its research goal, the
specific research objectives of the University are to promote
the quality and range of research undertaken
the research capacity of staff and students
the conduct of research relevant to social, cultural and
economic needs and intellectual advancement
the encouragement and support of creativity and
innovation.
Through its University Research and Innovation Committee, QUT devises an
annual University Research and Innovation Plan to guide and focus
University-wide planning around current critical issues and to
provide strategies by which these research objectives can be
achieved.
