QUT Staff Community Welfare Fund Committee is a Vice-Chancellor's committee established in accordance
  with 
Council Procedure 1 - Committees
.  QUT Staff Community
  Welfare Fund Committee is accountable to the Vice-Chancellor for fulfilling the following terms of
reference.
Terms of reference
QUT Staff Community Welfare Fund Committee:
oversees management of the QUT Staff Community Welfare Fund which supports community projects to
    address the human needs of those in the community where those needs are not being totally met by
    their own resources or government assistance;
raises money for the QUT Staff Community Welfare Fund from QUT staff members, in the form of payroll
    deductions or gifts;
advises the Vice-Chancellor on an annual basis on appropriate distribution of funds from the QUT
    Staff Community Welfare Fund.
Membership
One member of academic staff nominated by the Vice-Chancellor after receiving expressions of interest
One member of professional staff nominated by the Vice-Chancellor after receiving expressions of
    interest
One senior manager with financial expertise nominated by the Vice-Chancellor, as chair
A nominee of the Registrar is Secretary, usually a member of the Committee
Tenure and frequency of meeting
Terms of office are as detailed in section 6 of 
Council
  Procedure 1 - Committees
QUT Staff Community Welfare Fund Committee meets as required.
Reporting
QUT Staff Community Welfare Fund Committee reports to the Vice-Chancellor annually, usually in conjunction
  with advice on distribution of funds.
