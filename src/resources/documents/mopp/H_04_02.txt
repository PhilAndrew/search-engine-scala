4.2.1 Tenders and quotations 
In accordance with relevant financial legislation, the University calls for tenders in relation to specific projects, eg capital and major maintenance works (see 
H/5.1
and 
H/6.1
), and also in relation to specific categories of goods and services.
Further operational details of the tender process relating to facilities management projects at different levels are provided on the 
Facilities Management Web Page
Top 
4.2.2 Consultants and Contractors Selection Committee 
The Consultants and Contractors Selection Committee is a standing committee within Facilities Management, reporting to the Director, Facilities Management . The committee deals with and provides advice on all aspects relating to the procurement of works and services and acts in accordance with the University's purchasing policies.
Further details are available from the Project Procedures Kit on the 
Facilities Management Web Page
